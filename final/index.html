who we are

Happy Music DJ, formerly known as P & M Music, Inc. has been serving the best music entertainment in Chinatown, NY for over 30 years. The manager, Charlie Wong with his wife Alice Wu have been running their record store during the day, and providing DJ entertainment at night ranging from weddings, birthday parties, social events, charities, organizations, and more. With over 30 years of experience, it is guaranteed that your event will have top of the line technology to provide the best quality of music.
